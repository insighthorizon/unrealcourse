	Concept
	- escape the room
	- you are awakaned in a locked room, unable to escape
	- use environmental clues such as light and sound to determine what to do next
	- trigger pressure plates and solve puzzles to progress towards the exit

	Rules
	- no lose condition (player should just have feeling that he must get out of the room,
	or he's going to die here
	- do whatever you can
	- you win by exiting the room

	Requirements
	- Unreal's providet Starter Content pack (assets)
	- C++ code and Blueprint to encode behaviour
	- sound effects
	- sketches for layout of room(s)
	- sketches for how puzzles work
